Aims and Goals

The goal is to get the SSSC together in between our yearly sprints to spend 2 half days in discussions and talks that will be useful for proposal writing and software developing over the next 6 months. Day 1 will be talks. Day 2 will be a series of breakout room discussions and group project work.
